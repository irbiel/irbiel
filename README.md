## Isabel R. Bielat
<p> I am a PhD Candidate in Modern European History at the University of Virginia. My specializations in nineteenth-century political expression, empire and nation, and print culture have led me to pursue digital humanities projects that invite multidisciplinary scholarly approaches to networks of primary sources.</p>
<p>As most of my collaborative and individual digital humanities projects reside in institutional repositories, this website provides access to these projects and context for my contributions.</p>
